# ATA

> ATA is not an AI translator.

**ATA** is a Knowledge-driven Translation Workspace designed for real localization workflows.

Instead of simply translating text, ATA helps teams accumulate, manage, and reuse translation knowledge.

---

## Vision

Build a workspace where translation knowledge becomes a company asset.

---

## Current Status

🚧 ATA is currently under active development.

Version: **V0.1**

---

## Philosophy

- AI recommends.
- Users decide.
- Knowledge comes first.
- AI is the engine, not the center.
